How It Works

Simple steps to get the help you need or support others

Getting Help is Easy

1

Submit a Report

Fill out our simple online form describing your situation or the issue you've encountered. Include as much detail as possible to help us understand your needs.

Submit Report
2

We Review Your Case

Our dedicated team carefully reviews each submission within 24-48 hours. We assess the urgency and determine the best way to provide assistance.

3

Get Connected

We'll reach out to you via email or phone to discuss your situation and explain the support we can offer. We work with you to create a personalized action plan.

4

Receive Support

Based on your needs, we provide direct assistance, connect you with local resources, or mobilize our volunteer network to help resolve your situation.

Types of Support We Provide

Education Support

School fees assistance, supplies, tutoring, and enrollment guidance so learners stay on track.

Food Assistance

Groceries, meal kits, and emergency food support for individuals and families in need.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can request help?

Anyone in need can submit a report. We serve individuals, families, and communities facing various challenges. You don't need to meet specific criteria â€“ if you need help, reach out to us.

Is there a cost for services?

No! All our services are completely free. We're a nonprofit organization funded by donations and grants. We never charge for assistance.

How long does it take to get help?

Response times vary based on urgency. Emergency cases are reviewed within 24-48 hours. Other requests typically receive a response within 3-7 days.

What information do I need to provide?

Basic contact information and a description of your situation. The more details you provide, the better we can help. All information is kept confidential.

Can I report on behalf of someone else?

Yes! If you know someone who needs help, you can submit a report on their behalf. Just provide their contact information and details about their situation.

How can I support the foundation?

You can donate, volunteer your time, or spread awareness about our work. Every contribution, big or small, helps us serve more people.
