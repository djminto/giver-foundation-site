Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information under the Jamaica Data Protection Act, 2020

1. Who We Are

The Giver Foundation ("we", "our") is a nonprofit organization based in Jamaica. This policy explains how we process personal data in line with the Jamaica Data Protection Act, 2020 ("DPA").

2. What We Collect

  • Contact details: name, email, phone.
  • Report details: location, category, urgency, description, previous help, consent.
  • Donations: donor name, email, amount, method, timestamps (we do not process card payments on this site).
  • Testimonials: name, email, rating, story, publication consent.
  • Technical: basic device/browser info and site usage (logs/analytics) to keep the site secure and improve usability.

3. Why We Use Your Data

  • To review and respond to reports and assistance requests.
  • To manage donations and send confirmations/receipts.
  • To publish testimonials when consent is provided.
  • To communicate updates, only with your consent where required.
  • To secure and improve the site (fraud prevention, troubleshooting, analytics).

4. Lawful Bases (DPA)

  • Consent: testimonials publication, optional communications.
  • Contract/legitimate interest: processing reports, coordinating assistance, confirming donations.
  • Legal obligations: complying with Jamaican law and valid requests from authorities.

5. Sharing

  • EmailJS to send notifications and confirmations.
  • Service providers for hosting, security, and analytics.
  • Authorities or partners only when necessary to safeguard individuals or meet legal obligations.

6. International Transfers

Some providers (like EmailJS) may process data outside Jamaica. We aim to use providers with appropriate safeguards and standard protections.

7. Retention

  • Reports and assistance records: kept as long as needed to follow up, then minimized or deleted.
  • Donation records: retained as needed for compliance and audit.
  • Testimonials: retained while published or until you withdraw consent.
  • Backups/logs: held for security and troubleshooting for limited periods.

8. Your Rights (under the DPA)

You can request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection to certain processing, and withdrawal of consent. To exercise rights, email giverfoundation2025@gmail.com. We will respond per statutory timelines.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No system is 100% secure; please contact us if you suspect a breach.

10. Cookies and Analytics

If analytics or performance cookies are used, they are only for improving the site. You can disable cookies via your browser settings; some features may be limited.
